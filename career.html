Business development executive.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following.
  • Execute e-mail campaigns for generating fresh leads and nurture the existing connections
  • Identify potential customers through cold calling, create and close new business opportunities in line with the strategic direction of the company
  • Set appointments, listen to customer needs and sell most appropriate product
  • Build and maintain healthy relation with existing/prospective clients
  • Regular follow-ups with the prospects that have been identified as potential clients and who have shown interest to use our services
  • Customer database management including creating fresh database and constantly maintaining the data up to date.
  • Researches and understands prospects before making the call
  • Understanding Client's requirement and coordinating with Design Team for same.
  • Digital Marketing through Social Media Postings
  • Identify requirements from Job portals, LinkedIn etc.
  • Providing Management with Feedback
  • Perform various clerical or administrative functions, such as sending quotations,Invoices.
  • Keep up-to-date on products and competition
Desired Candidate Profile
  • Should have a degree in marketing - BMS / BMM / PGDM / MBA
  • Strong oral and written communication skills.
  • Should posses "Go getter" attitude.
  • Advanced level knowledge of computers, MS Office, Internet use.
  • Ability to establish/maintain credibility with customers and partners
  • Ability to influence and negotiate with customers.
Salary Package: 1.8-2.4 Lac Per Year Plus Incentives.
